Main applications and Use Cases

A unified platform to build, train, host, and experiment with AI — then commercialize it at scale. End-to-end tools that turn ideas into permissionless AI apps, tokenized agents, and on-chain services.

Open Al Model Hub

A shared repository of open-source Al models and datasets, allowing anyone to access, contribute, and build upon community-driven intelligence.

Global Al Deployment Layer

Launch Al apps, APIs, and agents instantly across the world, bypassing banks, approvals, or app store gatekeepers.

Decentralized Compute

A global GPU/CPU network (nodes) delivering scalable, low-cost compute power without reliance on centralized cloud providers.

Tokenization & Monetization of Al

Turn models, datasets, and services into on-chain assets with built-in payments, royalties, and revenue-sharing.

Hosting, Fine-Tuning & Training

Run, customize, and improve Al models directly on the network-tailored to specific industries, tasks, or datasets.

P2P Al Marketplace

A borderless exchange where creators publish Al services and users consume them directly - trustless, transparent, and censorship-resistant.
